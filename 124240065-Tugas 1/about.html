Lee Hi

About

Award-winning artist Lee Hi has made her mark in the K-Pop industry with her soulful voice and emotional depth. Rising to fame as the K-Pop Star runner-up, she topped charts with 1,2,3,4 and earned acclaim with Breathe. Winning awards like Best R&B Artist and Best Vocal Performance, she continues to shape Korean R&B under AOMG, proving her timeless artistry.
